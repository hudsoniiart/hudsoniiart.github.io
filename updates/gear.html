There is no such thing as too much gear.

Photo & Video Stuff

My main camera is a Nikon Z50 II with a 200-500mm Nikkor lens. I started with a Nikon Coolpix P1000.

I have a DJI RS 3 Mini gimbal that I use occasionally. And an old Manfrotto tripod.

Computer Programs

I use the awful NX Studio program for editing RAW files. It pales in comparison to Darktable, a free program that I highly recommend. For simple cropping, Paint.NET or the default Windows Photos program. For other sorts of edits, Krita, Inkscape and GIMP.

DaVinci Resolve for editing videos and also audio.
