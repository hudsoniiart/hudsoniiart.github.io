Computer Programs

I enjoy free software.

Photo Editing

I currently don't do much beyond cropping, though I know that editing can improve the quality of a photo. For cropping I use Paint.NET or the default Windows Photos program. For more artistic (or just adding text) edits I use Krita, Inkscape and GIMP.

Video Editing

I use DaVinci Resolve for editing videos. It's a fantastic program.
