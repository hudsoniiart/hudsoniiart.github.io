ABOUT ME

I'm working on the working on.

Hudsonii Art is a personal project for showcasing various things I've done. Primarily photography, some videos.

When not taking photos of things, I work in tech support for security cameras. I volunteer with the Westman Naturalists, a nature group based in Brandon, and post most of their posts on IG/FB. I am also a member of the Brandon Camera Club and Westman Wilderness Club.
