Hudsonii Art is a personal project for showcasing various things I've done. Primarily photography and videography.

When not taking photos of things, I'm editing/sorting photos of things. I volunteer with Westman Naturalists, a nature group based in Brandon, and create most of their posts on IG/FB. I also assist with the group newsletter and occasionally lead outings.

I like to partake in bird counts/blitzes organized by the Manitoba IBA Program. I'm a member of the Brandon Camera Club, which is a great group for learning about cameras from other photographers with years of experience. Other groups to check out in Brandon include the Assiniboine Food Forest Initiative and the Westman Wilderness Club.
