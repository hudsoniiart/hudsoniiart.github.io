Hudsonii Art is a personal project for showcasing various things I've done. Primarily photography and videography.

When not taking photos of things, I'm editing/sorting photos of things. I volunteer with Westman Naturalists, a nature group based in Brandon, and create most of their posts on IG/FB. I also assist with the group newsletter and occasionally lead outings.

The Manitoba IBA Program hosts bird blitzes and other outings across Manitoba. I'm a member of the Brandon Camera Club, which is a great group for learning about cameras from photographers with years of experience. Other groups to check out in Brandon include the Assiniboine Food Forest and the Westman Wilderness Club.

This is a logo. Created on 17 July, 2024.

Oh, and my name is Carson.
